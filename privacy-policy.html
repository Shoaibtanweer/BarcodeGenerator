Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Barcode Generator collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect any personal data from you when you use our website. We do not store any information you enter into the barcode data field. All barcode and QR code generation happens directly in your browser.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not use your information for any purpose.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

This website does not use cookies or any other tracking technologies to collect information about your Browse behavior or personal data. We only use local storage in your browser to remember your dark mode preference.

  • Local Storage for Dark Mode: We use your browser's local storage to save your preference for dark mode. This preference is stored on your device and is not transmitted to us.

4. Third-Party Services

We use third-party libraries (JsBarcode, QRCode.js, Tailwind CSS, Font Awesome) directly loaded from CDNs to enable the functionality and styling of our website. These services may have their own privacy policies. We do not transmit any of your input data to these third-party services.

5. Data Security

As we do not collect or store any personal data, there is no personal data for us to secure. Your input data for barcode generation remains on your device.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
